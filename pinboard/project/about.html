About The Archive

The Archive is a selection of Year 1 Graphic Design work at Camberwell. It is a collection of projects that have been created by students in the first year of their studies. The Archive serves as a showcase for the creativity and talent of the students, and it provides a glimpse into the diverse range of work that is being produced at Camberwell. The Archive is a valuable resource for students, educators, and anyone interested in graphic design, as it offers insights into the creative process and the development of ideas. It also serves as a source of inspiration for future projects and encourages students to push the boundaries of their creativity.

The Archive is not only a celebration of the work produced by Year 1 students but also a testament to the vibrant and dynamic community at Camberwell. It reflects the dedication and passion of the students, as well as the supportive environment fostered by the faculty. The Archive is an essential part of the Camberwell experience, providing a platform for students to share their work and connect with others in the field of graphic design.
