Our Story

The Archive is a digital culinary experience showcasing the creative ingredients of first-year Graphic Design students at UAL Camberwell.

We treated the curriculum like a kitchen. Unit 1 (Starters) represents the foundational appetisers of design; Unit 2 (Mains) is the hearty core of the development process; and Unit 3 (Desserts) provides the final outcomes.

We blended the nostalgia of the classic 1950s American Diner with the structure of a contemporary graphic design archive.
